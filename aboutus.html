About us

OnlyBenefits is a student discount application that offers access to student deals for students who participant in campus activities. Anyone who is a UWC student or faculty member can visit the site and see the activities and deals.

We aim to help UWC students make the most of campus life!

  • OnlyBenefits was created in 2022 for the initial set up at the University of the Western Cape. With future plans to expand to other universities in the Western Cape region.
  • OnlyBenefits provides benefits and activities all year round.
  • We are a team of dedicated individuals who are tryinh to make the lives of students cheaper and more involved.
